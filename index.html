Key Features

Unlock the power of task management with these powerful features, designed to keep you organized and boost your productivity.

Prioritize Like a Pro

Easily prioritize your tasks with our intuitive drag-and-drop interface. Organize tasks by urgency or importance and stay focused on what matters most.

Smart Task Scheduling

Schedule tasks seamlessly with our calendar integration. Set due dates, create recurring tasks, and receive timely reminders to ensure you never miss a deadline.

Effortless Task Breakdown

Break down complex tasks into smaller, manageable steps. Our subtask feature helps you tackle big projects with ease, ensuring steady progress toward your goals.

About Us

Our lives everyday is filled with a lot of activities that needs to be carried out especially for student like us learning FullStack Software Engineering. We often have too much to juggle with, from studies to practice, projects completion deadlines and other activities including personal stuffs. We have used some task management app that helped us archive some of our goals. So we thought why don't we design a task manager application tailored for students like us and in extension a wider user community. That was when we came up with the idea of a task manager application, and called it "Todos".

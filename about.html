About Us

We Are Good at What We Do


At Todos, our mission is to provide a powerful yet easy-to-use task management solution that fits seamlessly into your life. Founded in 2024, we recognized the need for a tool that not only helps you track tasks but also empowers you to manage them efficiently and effectively.


Our Mission:


Our goal is to transform the way people handle their tasks by offering a platform that combines simplicity with robust functionality. We believe that with the right tools, everyone can achieve their goals and enhance their productivity.

Our Story

Driven by passion for organization and efficiency, our team set out to develop a task management solution that caters to diverse needs. From brainstorming to development, we have focused on creating an app that is intuitive, adaptable, and reliable. Our team consists of an enthusiatic frontend developer and a committed backend developer, all committed to delivering an exceptional user experience.


Our Values:

  • Simplicity: We strive to make task management straightforward and easy to navigate.

  • Innovation: We continuously enhance our app with new features and improvements to keep you ahead of the curve.

  • User-Centric Design: We listen to our users and design features that meet real needs and challenges.

  • Reliability:Our app is built to be dependable and secure, ensuring your tasks are always within reach.
